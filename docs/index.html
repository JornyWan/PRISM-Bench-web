Overview

PRISM‑Bench is a puzzle‑based visual reasoning benchmark that evaluates multimodal LLMs along two complementary tracks:

  • Answer Prediction (VQA‑style): choose the correct option for a structured visual puzzle.
  • First‑Error Detection: given a step‑by‑step chain‑of‑thought (CoT), identify the first incorrect step.

Core features: annotated CoTs, single‑error CoT variants, category labels, and per‑step correctness tags. v0.1 contains ~1.6k+ curated items across diverse puzzle types.

Tasks & Annotations

  • Multiple‑choice answers with definitive ground truth.
  • Human‑vetted CoTs and single‑error CoT variants.
  • Per‑step labels enabling step‑level evaluation.

What It Measures

  • Visual‑logical consistency and reasoning robustness.
  • Sensitivity to subtle rule violations and distractors.
  • Separation of getting the answer vs. verifying the reasoning.

Intended Use

  • Benchmark MLLMs beyond final‑answer accuracy.
  • Train/evaluate verifiers and critic models.
  • Probe category‑specific failure modes.
